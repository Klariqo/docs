<Note>
  Status: planned. CRM integrations are not live yet. This page describes where they are headed so you know what to expect. In the meantime you can already receive every record through [compliance export webhooks](/compliance-export-webhooks/overview) and route it into your own systems, including your CRM.
</Note>

Klariqo will offer native integrations that push compliance records, QA outcomes, and call metadata into your CRM, so the evidence lands on the right contact or deal automatically.

## Planned

- GoHighLevel
- HubSpot
- Salesforce

Each integration appears here as a full setup guide when it ships. We do not publish a guide for an integration that is not live.

<Warning>
  A CRM integration exports evidence into your CRM. It does not change your compliance obligations, and it does not manage your CRM's own consent or contact settings.
</Warning>
