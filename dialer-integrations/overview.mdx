---
title: "Dialer integrations"
description: "How Klariqo sits on top of the dialer you already run, starting with VICIdial."
---

Klariqo connects to the dialer you already run. It does not replace your dialer, your campaigns, or your agents. It joins as a layer on top. Most customers connect for **compliance and QA**, where Klariqo reviews your calls from their recordings and produces signed records. Klariqo can also run its **AI voice agent** on your dialer.

## Connect your dialer
