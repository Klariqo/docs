Klariqo connects to the dialer you already run. It does not replace your dialer, your campaigns, or your agents. It joins as a layer on top, handling AI calls, reviewing calls for compliance, and producing signed records on your existing system.

## Connect your dialer

<Card title="VICIdial" icon="phone" href="/dialer-integrations/connect-vicidial" horizontal>
  Connect Klariqo to your VICIdial as a remote SIP agent. This is the primary path, set up by your VICIdial administrator.
</Card>

## Also supported

You can also bring your own Twilio number and point it at Klariqo, which suits inbound and simpler setups.

More dialers, including Genesys, Five9, 8x8, and RingCentral, are planned. Setup guides appear here as each one ships, so you will not find a guide for anything that is not live yet.
